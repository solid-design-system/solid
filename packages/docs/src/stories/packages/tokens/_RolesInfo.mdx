There are two roles when working with tokens: **Theme Host** and **Theme Consumer**. Your role depends on whether you own the page or are embedded inside someone else's page.
If you build a standalone page with your own components, you fulfill both roles — you load a theme as a Host and use tokens in your components as a Consumer.
