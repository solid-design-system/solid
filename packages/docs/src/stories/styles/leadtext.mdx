[Leadtext with Mark](/?path=/docs/templates-mark--docs)

### Common Use Cases

- Provide a short, high-impact introduction to the topic or a brief summary at the top of a page or at the beggining of a new section.
- Draw attention to a critical statement or focal message that sets the tone for the content below.

### Usage Guidelines

#### Content and Emphasis

- Keep leadtext brief for maximum visual impact; if it grows lengthy, break it into blocks to remain scannable.
- Use leadtext to highlight key information or a standout idea, rather than duplicating a heading.

#### Background

- Use light background options like white, neutral-100, primary-100, or use a primary background when inverted.

### Accessibility Information

- Confirm that leadtext is visually distinct from regular text to emphasise its introductory purpose without replacing headings.
- Avoid using leadtext as a structural heading; maintain proper semantic headings for section organization.
